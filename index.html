⚔️ About HabitQuest
What is HabitQuest?

HabitQuest is a free, gamified habit tracker that turns your daily routines into an RPG adventure. Instead of boring checkboxes, you earn XP, level up through 100 ranks, defeat daily boss monsters, complete quests, and unlock achievements — all by simply building better habits.

Everything runs directly in your browser. No account required, no data sent to any server — your habits are stored privately on your own device.

🎮
Gamified XP System
Earn XP for every habit completed. Level up across 100 ranks from Novice to Ascendant.
👹
Daily Boss Battles
A new boss appears every day. Defeat it by completing your habits and earn bonus rewards.
🔥
Streak Tracking
Build momentum with daily streaks. Watch your fire grow and protect it at all costs.
🏆
Achievements
Unlock badges and achievements as you hit milestones. Flex your progress in your profile.
📊
Stats & Analytics
Detailed charts and heatmaps let you see your habit patterns and consistency over time.
🔒
100% Private
No account. No cloud. No tracking. All data stays on your device. Export anytime as JSON.

🔒 Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2025
1. Overview

HabitQuest ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user. HabitQuest is a browser-based application available at realhabittracker.com.

2. Data We Collect

Locally Stored Data: All habit data, XP, streaks, profiles, and settings are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Data: Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. This is used solely for security and operational purposes.

3. Cookies

HabitQuest itself does not use tracking cookies. However, we use Google AdSense to display advertisements, which may place cookies on your device to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your browsing history. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's Advertising Policy. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website and other websites.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to power the app. These are loaded via CDN and may log request metadata per their own privacy policies: Chart.js (charting), Canvas Confetti (animations), and Tabler Icons (icon fonts). No personal data is shared with these services.

6. Children's Privacy

HabitQuest is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control. We recommend regularly exporting your data using the built-in backup feature. We do not have access to your habit data.

8. Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or using your browser's "Clear Site Data" option. Since no data is stored on our servers, there is no additional deletion process needed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of HabitQuest after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at manavkumarkaushik@gmail.com.
