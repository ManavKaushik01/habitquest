Frequently Asked Questions

Is HabitQuest really free?

Yes — the core experience is completely free. We're supported by non-intrusive advertising through Google AdSense.

Is my data private?

Your habit data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We never transmit your personal habit information to our servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I use HabitQuest offline?

Yes — install HabitQuest as a Progressive Web App (PWA) by tapping "Add to Home Screen" on mobile or using the install prompt in your browser.

I lost my data — can you recover it?

Because your data is stored locally in your browser, we don't have access to it and cannot recover it if it's lost. We recommend using the Export Backup feature regularly to save a copy of your data.
